Ma Po Toufu

This mapo tofu recipe (麻婆豆腐) is the true blue, authentic real deal—the spicy, tongue-numbing, rice-is-absolutely-not-optional, can’t-have-just-one-scoop mapo tofu that you get in the restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 ounces andouille sausage, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • one box of toufu

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place butter and sausage in a large stockpot over medium heat; cook and stir until sausage begins to brown, 5 to 6 minutes.
  3. Stir in paprika, cumin, and cayenne; cook for 1 minute.
